Grilled Cheese

How to Make Grilled Cheese :

  • - 2 peices of bread
  • - 2 pats of butter
  • - 1 slice of montery jack cheese
  • - 1 slice of aged cheddar cheese

Process :

  1. Fetch yourself a fat-bottom skillet
  2. Heat the skillet over meduim heat
  3. Butter one side of each slice of bread
  4. Place one slice of buttered bread , buttered side down , into the skillet
  5. Quickly place each slice of cheese on the bread , being sure to cover the entire area
  6. Place the other slice of bread , butter side up , on top of the cheese
  7. Wait approximately 1 to 2 mminutes ( till the bread touching the skillet is golden brown)
  8. Flip the sandwich over with some awesome pizzaz and cook the other side till golden brown ( approximately 1 to 2 minutes)
  9. Eat with ketchup , or just enjoy th plain crispy goodnes of your grilled cheese

Note : Grilled cheese is great on a grill , but for a quicker method , try using a clamshell panini press. The result will be much quicker, allowing you to make many more for you and your friends.

How to Make French Fries Crispy and Delicious!

Ingredients:

  • - 4 large potatoes
  • - a pot of boiling water
  • - a pot of oil heated to 350 degrees fahrenheit

Process:

  1. Cut the potatoes into strips , roughly a 1/4" by 1/4"
  2. Place the potato strips into the boiling water for approximately 2 minutes
  3. Remove them and let them dry, or pat them dry
  4. Once dry, place the strips into the hot oil
  5. Cook for approximately 8 minutes or until golden brown or crispy
  6. Serve on a plate with ketchup , vinegar , or if you're a complete weirdo , mayonnaise
